Consolidated Notes & Strategy

Notes
  • Rounding Rules: Check the digit immediately following the last required digit. If it is 5 or greater, round up (add 1). If it is less than 5, leave the digit unchanged (round down).
  • Significant Figures (s.f.): Start counting from the first non-zero digit. Leading zeros are not significant. Trailing zeros after a decimal point are significant.
  • Decimal Places (d.p.): Start counting immediately after the decimal point.

  1. The figure shows the quadrilateral ABCD. If ABC+ADC=ACD=BAC=90, which of the following must be true?

This matches Option C perfectly.

Conclusion: Option C must be true.

Notes & Strategy

Key Concepts:

  • Angle Sum of Triangle: The interior angles sum to 180. In a right-angled triangle, the two acute angles are complementary (sum to 90).
  • Similarity of Triangles (AA): If two angles of one triangle are equal to two angles of another, the triangles are similar.
  • Trigonometric Ratios: In a right-angled triangle, tan=OppositeAdjacent.

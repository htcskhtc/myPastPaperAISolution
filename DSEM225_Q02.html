Raw Notes from Solver

Notes:
  • Compound Angle Formula: tan(A+B)=tanA+tanB1tanAtanB
  • Special Value: tan(4)=1
  • Standard Limit: lim0sin=1
Strategy:
  1. In Part (a), recognize the tan(4+h) structure immediately. Expand it and carefully simplify the complex fraction by multiplying numerator and denominator by the reciprocal.
  2. In Part (b), "First Principles" means you must use the limit definition.
  3. Crucial Step: Directly substitute the expression derived in (a) into the limit numerator. Do not re-derive it.
  4. Factor out terms containing h in the denominator to cancel with the h from the definition, or isolate sinhh to apply limit laws.

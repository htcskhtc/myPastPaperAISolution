Consolidated Notes & Strategy

1. Notes

  • Slope Formula: For Ax+By=C, Slope = A/B.
  • Intercepts: Put x=0 to find y-intercept; Put y=0 to find x-intercept.
  • Signs: Slope upwards = Positive. Slope downwards = Negative. Above x-axis = Positive y-intercept.

2. Strategy

  • Visual Inspection: First, determine the signs (+/) of unknown constants (a,c) by observing the slope direction.
  • Steepness: Compare magnitudes. Steeper lines have slopes with larger absolute values.
  • Inequalities: Be extremely careful when multiplying inequalities by unknown constants. Always determine if the constant is negative first.

3. Required Skill

  • Algebraic Manipulation: Solving simultaneous linear equations to find intersection points.
  • Inequality Logic: Applying rules of inequalities (reversing signs) correctly.
  • Coordinate Geometry: Relating algebraic coefficients to geometric features (slope, position).

Question Code: DSE23PII_Q07

  1. If y=73.8 (correct to 3 significant figures), find the range of values of y.
    1. 73.7y<73.9
    2. 73.7<y73.9
    3. 73.75y<73.85
    4. 73.75<y73.85

Solution

Concept Note: Identifying the Scale Interval When a number is corrected to n significant figures, identify the place value of the n-th digit.
For 73.8:
  • 7 is the 1st significant figure (Tens).
  • 3 is the 2nd significant figure (Units).
  • 8 is the 3rd significant figure (Tenths, i.e., 0.1).
The "scale interval" is therefore 0.1.
Since y=73.8 correct to 3 significant figures, the 3rd significant figure is the tenths digit. Therefore, y is rounded to the nearest 0.1. Half of 0.1 is 0.05. So the true value of y must satisfy 73.80.05y<73.8+0.05.
Common Trap: Inequality Signs Remember the definition of rounding:
  • The lower bound is always inclusive () because 73.75 rounds UP to 73.8.
  • The upper bound is always exclusive (<) because 73.85 would round UP to 73.9, not 73.8.
Hence, 73.75y<73.85. Therefore, the correct option is C: 73.75y<73.85.

  1. The perimeter of the sector OMN is 12 cm, where O is the centre of the sector OMN. If the radius of the sector OMN is 3 cm, which of the following are true?

Check I. Area of the sector OMN

Alternative Formula for Area:
Instead of finding first, you can use A=12rs, where s is the arc length. Here, A=12(3)(6)=92. This is much faster!

The area of a sector is the same fraction of the area of the whole circle: Area of sector=360r2. Since 360=1 and r=3, Area of sector OMN=360(3)2=192=92 cm2. So the area is 92 cm2, and hence Statement I is true.

Question Code: DSE25PII_Q12
  1. It is given that z varies directly as the cube of x and inversely as the square of y. When x=3 and y=6, z=3. When x=5 and y=2, z=
    1. 5.
    2. 25.
    3. 125.
    4. 243.

Solution:

Concept Note Joint Variation: "Directly as A" means A is in the numerator. "Inversely as B" means B is in the denominator. Always introduce a constant k0.

Since z varies directly as the cube of x and inversely as the square of y, we can write

z=kx3y2,

where k is a constant of proportionality.

Common Trap Read Carefully: Ensure you use "cube" (x3) and "square" (y2) correctly. A common mistake is swapping the powers or neglecting them.

Using the given data x=3, y=6 and z=3,

3=k3362=k2736=k34.

Hence,

k=343=4.

Therefore, the relationship is

z=4x3y2.

When x=5 and y=2,

z=45322=41254=125.

Thus, z=125, so the correct option is (C).

Notes & Strategies:

Topic: Variation (Joint Variation)

  • The general form for "z varies directly as xn and inversely as ym" is z=kxnym.
  • Always find the constant k first using the initial set of values provided.
  • Be careful with arithmetic when dealing with fractions and powers.

Consolidated Notes & Strategy

Notes (Memorize)
  • Variation Formula: If z varies directly as A and inversely as B, then z=kAB.
  • Constant k: Never forget the variation constant k. It acts as the scaling factor bridging the variables.
  • Powers: "Square" = x2, "Cube" = x3, "Square root" = x.
Strategy (Procedure)
  1. Translate: Convert the English sentence into a mathematical equation involving k.
  2. Substitute (Find k): Plug in the first set of numbers (complete set) to solve for k.
  3. Rewrite: Write the specific formula with the found value of k.
  4. Solve: Plug in the remaining values to find the unknown variable.

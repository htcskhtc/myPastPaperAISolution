Step-by-Step Solution
1. Linearization of the Equation
To relate the equation y=mxn to the linear graph of logay against logax, we must take the logarithm to base a on both sides.

(1)y=mxn(2)logay=loga(mxn)(3)logay=logam+logaxn(4)logay=nlogax+logam
Let Y=logay and X=logax. The equation becomes: Y=nX+logam This is the equation of a straight line (Y=MX+C) where:
  • Slope (M) = n
  • Y-intercept (C) = logam

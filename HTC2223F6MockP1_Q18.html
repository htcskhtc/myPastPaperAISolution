AI-Generated Notes & Strategies

Note on Folding Problems: When a 2D shape is folded, the dimensions (lengths and angles) within the rigid faces (here ADC and ABC) remain unchanged. The only thing that changes is the 3D relationship (dihedral angle) between the faces. Always identify which lengths/angles are preserved.

Note on 3D Angles: To find the angle between a line (MC) and a plane (Ground), you must identify the projection of the line onto the plane (MC). Construct the right-angled triangle (MMC) formed by the point, its projection, and the intersection with the plane.
